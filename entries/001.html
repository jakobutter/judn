entry #001

     i have accepted the nature of my demise, i have accepted my mortality. i strive to not only live off of my creation, but to transcend and live through my work vicariously. everything we love will disintegrate. but do i really give a shit? what good is weighing yourself down with mortal dielemmas that you will not live to experience? im on FL Studio and Hammer Editor... doing what i love
