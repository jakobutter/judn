     ive been an avid source engine & valve fanatic since i was eleven years old. when i was twelve i dug my grubby fingers into mapmaking, which i was very shit at. i cant even use the excuse of "i was 12" cause, ive seen good maps made by young people.

     i never put as much time into source as i did other hobbies like music. still it is something i have a deep-rooted passion for. any intermediate-level source fanatic will be able to pick apart these maps and find a number of issues with them. please excuse my ignorance.

     the textures were made in many different ways. some were ai-generated. some painted by hand. some generated via software. eh, maybe some ripped ones too. same goes for the models, fuck knows where they came from. i ripped them directly from other maps. LOL!

     shoutout to you if your textures/models ended up in my map, they were stolen with good intentions ❤️ i hope you enjoy these maps, feel free to pick them apart and openly cricicize my ameteur mapmaking skills.
